# Detailed Review Workflow

Use this workflow when the user wants defense questions for a thesis in Word or PDF format and does not need written review comments yet.

Procedure: read the paper; extract title, abstract, chapter structure, conclusion, and references; identify the real thesis task; locate vulnerable academic points; sample modern scholarship for AI-heavy drafting signals; produce a basic understanding, main problems, and exactly 3 defense questions.

High-value oral-defense questions require the student to explain why a chapter exists, justify key interpretation, connect a cited scholar to a specific paragraph, distinguish summary from argument, defend modern concepts on ancient material, or explain why a teaching chapter belongs in the thesis.

For each major problem, identify the smallest useful close-reading target: chapter, subsection, transition, table, literature-review paragraph, or key concept paragraph.

AI verification checks: author, title, publication/institution, year/issue, abstract/summary when available; then ask whether the source is actually used, accurately described, and necessary to the body argument.
